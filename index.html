Fresh from the house.

I just released another application to Google Play called "Hangman Azul". The purpose is in the name, it's a hangman game! However the azul portion bears significance as well. Azul is spanish for blue, and that color would be the basis for the theme of this app. The background is blue and the usage is through bluetooth. The bluetooth part is the primary function, as this is only a multiplayer game. I attempted to start something like this over a year ago, but was unable to realize it. Earlier this year I revisited the idea and was able to finally get it set up! It's currently at an early release, at Version 0.2.1, but it has been in the works for some time now.

You can check it out for yourself in the Google Play store here.

Welcome to Hangman Azul!


Hangman Azul is a multiplayer bluetooth application, allowing you to enjoy the game with your friends.

Word choices are up to you! Being bluetooth you can use any words you and your partner feel like. After all, it is a game between friends.

The carefully built look is custom from head to toe. From the individual words, to the selection tray and the animating background. This was meant to be something you can enjoy not only as a game but as a visual work.

This is an early release (V 0.2.1) and will be continued to be improved upon as time goes. Expect good things, and enjoy!

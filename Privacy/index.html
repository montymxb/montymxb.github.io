Uphouseworks Privacy Policy


Uphouseworks is run an maintained as a non-advertising site, and does not collect information on you for selling to advertising or other such related purposes. Your information is private and remains so.


uphouseworks.com is hosted by Github. Any collection on their part is beyond the scope and will be ultimately unaddressed in this policy for this site. For more information visit github.com and see their privacy policy regarding hosted sites.


Google Analytics is utilized strictly to determine and better understand to traffic to and from this website. Any information that may be collected about you is not used in any way to personally identify, market, or otherwise monetize you. Your privacy is important, and your information is never shared with anyone beyond the owner and operator of this site, Benjamin Wilson Friedman. The use of your information is used exclusively for understanding users and their behavior on this site in order to provide a more satisfying experience for current and future users.


This site does not provide an ssl or tsl protocol via which to access it (https), and as such your traffic may be visible to others around you. In the case this concerns you do not visit this site without using a proxy of some kind. You can utilize a proxy service such as tor to hide your traffic if so desired.


For any other concerns you may contact the owner and operator at ben@axolsoft.com.
